ABOUT ME

Welcome to my website. My name is Rohan Chandra. I am a senior pursuing electronics and communication at Delhi Technological University, New Delhi, India. My hobbies are playing chess and ‘mathemagics’. The latter is a just a fancy term for vedic math or speed math. My favorite game is the one where you take apart the number from the number plate of the car in front of you and multiply it in your head. My record is 13 cars in 56 seconds. So the take away is you probably shouldn’t get into a car with me.

I am interested in studying artificial intelligence and algorithms. But to make use of my ECE background, I want to include a flavour of hardware too, so I shall also be studying a bit of robotics. So basically, I want to be somewhere in the intersection of AI, algorithms and robotics!
