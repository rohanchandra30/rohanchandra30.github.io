Mask-RVO: A Robust and Novel Pedestrian Tracker for Highly Dense Crowds



We present a new pedestrian tracking architecture that works in highly dense settings. Our architecture comprises of a non-linear motion model and uses detections computed using Mask-RCNN. We use many different datasets including the standard MOT benchmark and non standard but much more highly dense datasets. We achieve state of the art results in reducing the number of false negatives and highly competitive results in most other metrics. Our tracker is generalized to perform well in videos of varying properties such as density, camera angle, camera motion, FPS, and lighting. Our tracker is online and works in real-time.
