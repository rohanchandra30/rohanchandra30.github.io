Pedestrian Tracking



SORT and its variant, Deep SORT, is a tracking by detection algorithm. Each object is associated with a linear constant velocity model. Associations between detections in successive frames is done by maximizing IOU of predicted and measured bounding boxes by using the Hungarian algorithm. In a more recent verison of this method, the authors have also used 2 additional metrics - the cosine metric, and the mahalanobis distance metric which help

The issue with using a linear constant velocity model is that they may not work in highy dense crowds. My research attempts to use a non linear motion model to track people in dense settings. Moreover, the performance of tracking-by-detection schemes depends hugely on the object detections at hand. I am thus also attempting to use improved detections.
