Introduction

I am a second year Masters student in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park. My academic advisor is Tom Goldstein. I am broadly interested in solving non-convex optimization problems. Currently, I'm trying to solve low rank matrix estimation in the natural parameter space without lifting to higher dimenions.

Previously, I worked on Phase Retrieval. Over the past few months, we created Phasepack, a MATLAB based library to solve phase retrieval using classical and contemporary routines. You can contact me if you wish to take a look at the unofficial versions of the paper and code. Final official versions are going to be up soon!
